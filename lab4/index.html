How to start a dirtbike:

    1) Put the key in the ignition. 2) Switch key to the on position. 3) Hold the clutch and switch the gear lever into neutral position. 4) Engage the choke. 5) Start engine. 6) Wait for the engine to warm up. 7) Disengage choke. 8) While holding the clutch, switch the bike into first gear. 9) Slowly release the clutch while accelerating.
